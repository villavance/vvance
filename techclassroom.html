Technologies in the Classroom

This course focuses on the different technology educators can use in the classroom. Every week or two, one type of technology/media will be explored, learn, and practice. The student will use the technology to create a meaningful project to understand the concept, have a practice time, and take ownership of the process. The objective is to help the student learn how to use the technology to incorporate in their classroom. The course will run for 16 weeks.
