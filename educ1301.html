EDUC 1301 Department Template

A collaborative project with Professor Gloria Garcia to transform her course into a department template so other faculty can use it. The end goal of the project is to create a better structure, add more student engagement, and turn the template into a department template. The course was designed for a 16-weeks semester and later on added another template for an 8-weeks semester. 

Technologies and application used for video materials. 
■ Powtoon - Instructor welcome video
■ Canva - Learning Objectives
■ After Effects - Tips for Being a Successful Online Student. Video animation and graphics by Samantha Rabino
